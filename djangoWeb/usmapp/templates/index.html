Welcome to University of Southern Maine (USM)

About USM

The University of Southern Maine (USM) is a public university with campuses in Portland, Gorham, and Lewiston in the state of Maine, United States.

USM offers undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields of study, including arts, sciences, business, education, health professions, and more.

USM Campuses

Portland Campus

The Portland campus is located in the heart of Maine's largest city and offers a vibrant urban atmosphere with access to cultural attractions, restaurants, and more.

Gorham Campus

The Gorham campus is situated in a suburban setting and offers a traditional college experience with a close-knit community and beautiful surroundings.

Lewiston Campus

The Lewiston campus offers a variety of programs and resources to serve students in the Lewiston-Auburn area and beyond.
